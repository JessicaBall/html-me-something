Allura Fette ☥ Performance Artist

Burlesque, Belly Dance, Fire Dance & Flow

A Versatile Performer

Allura Fette has an extensive repertoire of acts ranging from classic to subversive including Nerdlesque, fetish and kink-centric, dark fusion, pop-culture characters, sultry striptease, over-the-top props, and themes to fit any party or event.

Her interests are widely varied, and drawn upon for inspiration in her acts. Burlesque-bellydance fusion takes the traditional elements of each form and marries them into captivating dances. Various flow tools such as hoop, fire fans, palm torches, and fire orb combined with other dance styles bring performances to a new level.

Acts can be adapted to stages large and small, from concert venues to intimate gatherings, and everything in between.

An Experienced Entertainer

With a background of over seven years as a burlesque performer and many years in theater, Allura is at home on the stage. Notable appearances include:

  • Main Stage of St. Louis PrideFest 2013
  • Hot & Heavy Burlesque in Chicago, IL
  • Show Me Burlesque and Vaudeville Festival 2011, 2013, 2015 & 2016
  • Conventions:
    • Conflation 2011, 2012, 2013, 2015 & 2017
    • Archon 2010 (Guerilla Burlesque)
    • Con-Tamination 3D 2012
  • Naughti Gras IX
  • St. Lou Fringe Festival 2013

Hold That Pose!

While art in motion is a fantastic experience, physical art can be enjoyed indefinitely.

Allura has over 15' years experience in art modeling for photography, sketching, and painting. She is available for drawing groups, or long-term projects for an artist to sculpt or paint.

Her photography modeling is extensive, and she is available for alternative, glamour, artistic or implied nude, budoir, fashion, print, commerical, promo, and pin-up modeling.
